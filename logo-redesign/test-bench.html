Notes.

Fonts loaded from Google Fonts at runtime: Fraunces (variable, optical sizing) for the wordmark; Geist for the descriptor. The wordmark uses weight 500 with letter-spacing of 0.05em as a starting point. The descriptor uses Geist 500 with heavy tracking (0.5em) for the small-caps look.

This is reference-grade type setting, not a finished mark. The next refinement passes need to address: optical kerning between R-T and T-N, the cap height ratio between wordmark and descriptor, the exact tracking of the descriptor, and the 16/32px favicon letterforms (which need to be drawn rather than scaled).
